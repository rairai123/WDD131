Overview

Purpose

CineCritic aims to provide movie enthusiasts with an interactive platform to discover, review, and discuss films. Our site offers a unique experience where users can hover over movie thumbnails to instantly preview clips, making the browsing experience more engaging and informative.

Audience

Our target audience includes movie lovers of all ages, from casual viewers to film critics. We cater to those who appreciate a visually appealing and interactive way to explore cinema, and who value community discussions about films.

Dynamic Elements

Our site will utilize JavaScript to create an engaging user experience: 1. Hover-to-Preview: When users hover over a movie thumbnail, a short clip will play, giving a quick preview of the film. 2. Interactive Search: A dynamic search bar will provide real-time results as users type. 3. Rating System: Users can rate movies, with the average rating updating in real-time. 4. Comment Section: A live-updating comment section for movie discussions. 5. Personalized Recommendations: Based on user's viewing history and ratings, we'll use algorithms to suggest new movies.
